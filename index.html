Ocean of Your Heart

Original Composition by Abu Sayed
Lyrics
[Verse 1]
Your eyes like streetlights in the dark,
Guiding me home to where you are.
I trace the shadows of your name,
And every heartbeat feels the same.
You paint the world in colors new—
I’m just a canvas made for you.
[Chorus]
Take me deeper, let the waves start,
Drowning in the ocean of your heart.
Every whisper, every spark,
I’m lost but found in where you are.
Hold me close, don’t let me part—
I’ll swim forever in your heart.
[Verse 2]
Your laugh’s a spark that lights the breeze,
A melody that bends the trees.
The stars align when you’re nearby,
Your voice the map, I’m learning why.
In every scar, in every rhyme,
You’re the peace I’ve chased through time.
[Bridge]
If time could freeze this moment’s glow,
Would we let the universe just know?
A thousand sunsets couldn’t compare
To the way your hands heal every tear.
[Outro]
So let the tide pull us afar,
No horizon’s wide enough to mar
This love that bends but won’t break apart—
I’ll swim forever in the ocean of your heart.
